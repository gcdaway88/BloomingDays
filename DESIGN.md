## Design System Overview

This document outlines the core aesthetic and functional principles of our user interface.

### Color Palette

Our palette is inspired by natural, muted tones, evoking a sense of calm and sophistication.

* **Primary Color:** `#87917D` - A grounding, earthy green, used for primary calls to action and brand accents.
* **Secondary Color:** `#B8938F` - A warm, muted rose, complementing the primary and providing visual interest.
* **Tertiary Color:** `#D4C4A8` - A soft, creamy beige, used for highlights and subtle decorative elements.
* **Neutral Color:** `#62625B` - A deep, cool grey, providing a stable foundation for backgrounds and text.

Our interface operates in a **light** mode, providing a crisp and clear visual experience.

### Typography

We employ a thoughtful selection of fonts to ensure readability and establish a distinct brand voice.

* **Headlines:** `notoSerif` - Chosen for its elegant and authoritative presence, ideal for titles and headings.
* **Body Text:** `manrope` - A clean, modern sans-serif that ensures high readability for longer passages.
* **Labels:** `manrope` - Consistent with body text for clarity and uniformity in interactive elements.

### Shape and Form

Our UI elements feature **subtly rounded** corners, offering a gentle softness without sacrificing professionalism.

### Spacing

We prioritize a **normal** level of whitespace, providing ample room for elements to breathe without feeling overly sparse or cramped. This balance enhances readability and user comfort.
