Flowers are most beautiful — and most affordable — when they're in season. Here's a quick guide to what's typically available and when, so you can plan your orders accordingly.

## Warm Season (March – August)

Expect an abundance of **sunflowers**, **gerbera daisies**, **gladiolus**, **zinnias**, and tropical varieties like **heliconia** and **bird of paradise**. This is also prime season for most garden roses.

## Cool Season (September – February)

Cooler months bring in **chrysanthemums**, **dahlias**, **alstroemeria**, and the beloved **roses** at their best quality. It's also the best time for **lavender** and many white blooms.

## Year-Round

Some stalwarts are available regardless of season: **carnations**, **lilies**, **anthuriums**, and most imported roses. These form the backbone of many of our standard arrangements.

## Why it matters

In-season flowers are fresher, last longer, and support local growers. When you order with us, we always try to incorporate seasonal varieties — it's part of how we keep every arrangement feeling alive and current.
