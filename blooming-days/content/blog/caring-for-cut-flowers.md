Getting a beautiful bouquet is just the beginning. With the right care, your arrangement can stay vibrant for up to two weeks. Here's what we recommend:

## The moment you receive them

Trim about 2–3 cm off the bottom of each stem at a 45-degree angle — this increases the surface area for water absorption. Do this under running water or in a bowl of water to prevent air bubbles from blocking the stem.

## Water and vase care

Fill a clean vase with cool, fresh water. Change the water every two days and re-trim the stems each time. Avoid placing your arrangement in direct sunlight, near fans, or beside ripening fruit (which releases ethylene gas and shortens flower life).

## Temperature matters

Flowers prefer cooler temperatures. If you can, move the arrangement to a cool room overnight — this significantly extends its life.

## Remove wilted flowers promptly

One wilting stem can accelerate the decline of the whole bunch. Remove damaged or wilted flowers as soon as you notice them.

## The flower food packet

If we included a flower food sachet with your order, dissolve it in the water. It contains sugar (food), an acidifier (to help absorption), and a biocide (to prevent bacterial growth) — all three work together to keep your flowers fresher, longer.
